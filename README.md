# 📚 AI Trainer & Annotator Course

A self-directed course built to develop foundational knowledge in AI annotation and AI trainer workflows. Created as part of a personal learning journey to transition into the AI data annotation field.
[https://freyton7.github.io/AI-Annotation-Practice-Task/](https://freyton7.github.io/AI-Trainer-and-Annotator-Course/)
---

## 📌 About This Project

This course was independently designed and built to cover the core concepts required in real-world AI data labeling and RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) workflows — without formal employment experience in the field.

---

## ✅ What It Covers

- Introduction to AI annotation and data labeling
- Text classification, sentiment labeling, and intent tagging
- AI Trainer fundamentals and RLHF concepts
- Ranking and evaluating AI-generated responses
- Annotation guidelines, quality control, and edge case handling

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This course was created to:
- Build foundational AI annotation knowledge independently
- Understand real AI trainer workflows and responsibilities
- Demonstrate initiative and readiness for AI annotation roles

- Claude AI (as learning and course development platform)
